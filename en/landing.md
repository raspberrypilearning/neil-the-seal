Neil the seal is loose in town, and he's out to cause chaos! In this project, you'll code a game where Neil waddles around smashing every sign, car, and barrier he can find, scoring points as he goes — all while a Ranger chases after him, trying to send him back to the sea.

Have a play with the finished game to see what you'll make.
